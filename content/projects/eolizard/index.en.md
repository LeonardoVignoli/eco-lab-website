## The project

EOLIZARD is a research and conservation project dedicated to the study of
the spatial structure, behaviour, and population dynamics of lizards in both
natural and human-modified environments.

## Aims
- population monitoring
- in situ and ex situ conservation
- scientific outreach and dissemination
