Leonardo Vignoli is Associate Professor of Zoology at Roma Tre University and head of the

LEC laboratory.



His research focuses on amphibian and reptile ecology, with particular interest in

population dynamics, behavioural ecology, functional ecology and conservation biology.



He is involved in in situ and ex situ conservation projects, long-term monitoring

programmes and the development of innovative approaches such as biologging and eDNA.
