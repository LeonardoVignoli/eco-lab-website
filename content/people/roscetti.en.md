I am a PhD student at the University of Rome Tor Vergata and collaborate with the LEC laboratory at Roma Tre University. My research focuses on the thermal ecology of amphibians and reptiles and on how these animals interact with the environmental conditions they encounter in their habitats.

I am interested in understanding how temperature variation affects individual behaviour, activity and decision-making, and how these responses differ among species and environments. By combining fieldwork and data analysis, I aim to improve our understanding of how ectotherms respond to environmental change and to provide useful knowledge for their conservation.
