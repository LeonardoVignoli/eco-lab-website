I'm a biologist with an interest in an interdisciplinary approach of ecology and evolutionary biology and fascinated by exploring patterns in data. During my past research, I combined animal biologging with mechanistic niche modelling to get insights on the interaction between animal functional traits and their habitat use. Currently, through my PhD in a LIFE project for the conservation of the endangered _Podarcis raffonei_, I am developing a research framework to unravel the mechanisms behind its decline.
