I am a biologist with a PhD in Zoology, and my research focuses on the ecology, demography, and conservation biology of herpetofauna. During my PhD, I studied native and non-native lizard populations in the Aeolian Archipelago to gather essential information for defining a conservation strategy for _Podarcis raffonei_. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher affiliated with the LEC, Roma Tre University, and CNR-IRET, where I work on the conservation of _Podarcis raffonei_ within the LIFE EOLIZARD project, contributing to the captive breeding and population monitoring of Aeolian wall lizards and the control of invasive rats and lizard populations.
